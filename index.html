About Our OMI WATER PURIFICATION SYSTEM

A little about us and a breif history of how we started.

Water is the fundamental part of our life. It could be for drinking, bathing or using in any kinds of industries. We cannot think a single moment of our life without water. From very beginning Omi Water Purification System has grown to become a world leader in design, drawing, supervising and supply of water & wastewater treatment and water desalination system.

Has founded in 2014 with an initial focus on Environmental Engineering segment. OMI WATER came to the Bangladesh attention in Environmental Segment followed by its work and experience expertise.
